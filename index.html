How it works
Rules are inserted at position 1 (top) of each chain, before UFW rules, ensuring all packets are captured.

Logs are written to /var/log/iptables.log via rsyslog.

Toggle switches add/remove -j LOG rules. The LOG target never drops packets — it only copies metadata.
